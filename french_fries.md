# French Fries

## Ingredients
* Potatoes
* Vegetable oil (preferably olive oil)
* vinegar
* salt

## Instructions
* Peel the potatoes, then place them in cold water
* Cut the potatoes in strips, about as thick as your little finger; place in cold water again.
* Rinse the potato strips.
* Bring cold water and vinegar to a boil, then simmer the potato strips for 10 minutes.
* drain and dry the strips
* preheat oil to 200°C to a depth of ca. 3cm
* fry the potato strips in batches for 50s, making sure they don't crowd too much.
* Remove with strainer, spread on a paper towel to drain. Let them cool down for 30min.
* Pre-heat oil to 200°C again.
* Re-fry the potato strips for about 4 minutes, or to the preferred level of crispiness. You can use larger batches than before.
* Remove with a strainer and let them strain in a bowl lined with paper towels.
* Salt to taste, serve hot.
